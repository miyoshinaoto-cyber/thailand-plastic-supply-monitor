This tool monitors Thailand-local news and regional/global upstream supply-chain signals that may affect Thailand's plastic resin, petrochemical, and lab consumables supply. Articles do not need to mention Thailand to qualify — naphtha price surges, polymer plant shutdowns, and resin shortages anywhere upstream can hit Thailand's supply. Data is refreshed daily by GitHub Actions; the dashboard reads ./data/signals.json.
